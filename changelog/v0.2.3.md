## Query Safety

Blocked patterns:
- `DELETE` / `UPDATE` without `WHERE` clause
- Tautological `WHERE` conditions (`1=1`, `'a'='a'`, `TRUE`)
- `OR` injection patterns (`OR 1=1`)
